Our Programs

Empowering youth through sports, education, and leadership

Youth Sports League

Ages 6-18. Learn teamwork, discipline, and athletic skills through soccer, basketball, and baseball.

Season: Spring & Fall (8 weeks each)

Locations: 5 community centers across the city

Includes: Uniform, equipment, and certified coaches

Cost: Free for families in need (sponsored by donors)

Leadership Academy

Weekly workshops and summer camps focused on public speaking, conflict resolution, and team building.

Duration: 12-week program + 1-week summer intensive

Topics: Communication, problem-solving, goal setting, mentoring

Certification: Leadership certificate upon completion

Mentorship Program

One-on-one mentoring with local professionals, college students, and community leaders.

Commitment: 2 hours per week for 6 months

Activities: Career exploration, homework help, life skills

Benefits: Scholarship opportunities and college prep

Community Outreach

Volunteer opportunities, food drives, and neighborhood clean-up events for youth and families.

Events: Monthly volunteer days, holiday giving, park restoration

Impact: Over 5,000 community members served annually

Recognition: Presidential Volunteer Service Award eligible

Game Changers – breaking barriers, building champions. Because every kid deserves the joy of sport.
